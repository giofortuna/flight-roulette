What is Dispatch?

Dispatch generates random, realistic flight assignments for flight simulator pilots. Pick your simulator and preferences, and the app selects an aircraft, airline, departure airport, and destination — constrained by real-world performance data.

Every generated flight is sent directly to SimBrief for a full operational flight plan, complete with routing, weather, and fuel calculations.

How it works
  • An airline is picked randomly from a database of ~400 worldwide operators
  • An aircraft is selected, filtered by your chosen simulator and flight type
  • A departure airport is chosen at random — restricted to airports with active commercial service by default, or the full ICAO database when set to All
  • A destination is picked within aircraft range and minimum runway requirements
  • Under Options: payload (passengers + cargo) can be randomly generated and passed to SimBrief, and the airport pool can be switched between Commercial and All
